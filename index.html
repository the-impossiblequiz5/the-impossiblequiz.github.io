The Impossible Quiz is an online trivia quiz with more game content suitable for multiplayer, online play, the impossible quiz play, unlimited play on Chromebook, and no in-game ads. It features only very hard questions with double meanings, tricks, and puns that require you to think outside the box. Out of the four answers, only one answer is right. There are in total 110 questions. You're given only three lives, and you'll lose one with every mistake you make. This original game works without Flash on any device.

Did you answer all 110 questions? Play The Impossible Quiz 2. Click the right answer out of the four options. Use Skips to skip a particular question of the game. Watch out for Bombs which have a timer to answer the question before the time runs out or it is Game Over.

  6. Shallots
  7. An elephant
  8. Find the green tick
  9. Top right 'THAT ONE'
  10. Dentures
